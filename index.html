TOKYO (東京)

Japan’s capital city with top schools and countless job opportunities.

Tokyo is Japan’s bustling capital, offering world-class educational institutions and endless opportunities for international students. It is a global hub for business, technology, and culture, providing a dynamic environment for students to grow academically and professionally.

YOKOHAMA (横浜)

Modern, peaceful city close to Tokyo with a good balance of life and study.

Yokohama is a vibrant, modern city just south of Tokyo. It offers a calm living environment while being close to Japan’s capital, making it ideal for students seeking both educational opportunities and a peaceful lifestyle.

OSAKA (大阪)

Friendly people, great food, and many part-time job options.

Osaka is famous for its warm locals, delicious cuisine, and vibrant student life. The city offers many part-time job opportunities, making it a practical and lively destination for international students.

KYOTO (京都)

Cultural city filled with history and top universities.

Kyoto is the historical and cultural heart of Japan. Known for its temples, traditions, and top universities, it provides an enriching environment for students who value education and cultural experiences.

NAGOYA (名古屋)

Industrial center with affordable living and good vocational programs.

Nagoya is a major industrial city offering excellent vocational programs and affordable living. It combines modern infrastructure with cultural experiences, making it ideal for students interested in education and industry.

FUKUOKA (福岡)

Warm weather, growing job market, and student-friendly environment.

Fukuoka is known for its mild climate, welcoming atmosphere, and student-friendly amenities. It’s a growing hub for international students seeking balance between study, work, and lifestyle.

SENDAI (仙台)

Calm, green city ideal for focused study and peaceful living.

Sendai is a green and peaceful city in northern Japan, perfect for students seeking focus and a relaxed lifestyle. It provides good educational facilities while maintaining a calm and safe environment.

KOBE (神戸)

Beautiful port city with a welcoming international community.

Kobe is famous for its scenic port views, multicultural community, and vibrant lifestyle. The city is student-friendly and offers a variety of cultural experiences along with academic opportunities.

CHIBA (千葉)

Coastal city close to Tokyo, offering modern life and great study options.

Chiba provides a balance of city convenience and coastal relaxation. With proximity to Tokyo, affordable living, and strong educational institutions, it’s an ideal spot for students and professionals alike.

SAITAMA (埼玉)

Quiet and safe area near Tokyo, perfect for study and part-time work.

Saitama is a calm and secure city near Tokyo, offering convenience for commuting and part-time jobs. It’s ideal for students seeking a balance between safe living and access to metropolitan opportunities.

Short Introduction

The word “Nakama” means companion or trusted friend in Japanese — a name that perfectly reflects our commitment to supporting students throughout their journey.
